About Us

Everything started with three young entrepreneurs who dream to make a difference for small businesses wishing to expand their borders.

Background

After years of witnessing the fast-growing China economy, we see a huge demand for raw materials and food that sometimes couldn’t be fulfilled by the local suppliers. Latin America has exactly the capability to provide a stable supply.

Our goal

Our goal is to make import & export easier and and to create a secure and transparent platform dedicated to help all those small businesses to develop, improve and become exporters of their high quality products.

Our service

We not only provide critical China-Latin America trade information and multilingual communications, help small businesses could keep growing and improving their services and products.

Our vision

To be the bridge between China and Latin America and a trustworthy, stable, and strong platform to strengthen the business relationship between China and Latin America.
